About Us

A little about Us

Welcome to blanket smile

Blanket Smile is a community based organization which includes many donation services like BlanketDonor, Gou Daan and Kabutar Daan. Blanket donation and Gou Daan can be donated using our secure online payment gateway. Kabutar Daan service will be soon available depending on number of request raised. BlanketDonor which helps poor people by donating a blanket in the winter season. We are basically intermediary organization between people who want to donate and people who need blanket. we are currently operating in Punjab region and looking forward to work in other parts of the country. People can donate a blanket from our website with the help of different payment methods available. we have different qualities of blankets, starting from Rs:199 to Rs:500

Facts about Blanket Donation

The rise in air pollution levels and the early onset of winter this year have made homeless people vulnerable, but at the same time it has given us an opportunity to serve humanity by donating blankets to the needy. we collect all the money from your donations and buy new blankets depending on the quality of blanket you choose. Every Saturday we reach out to people who don't have a shelter live and donate them blankets as per their requirements. We will capture the moment of happiness created by your donation. Our team will capture the pictures of those people who got blanket and we will share it on your mail within next24hours of donation.
